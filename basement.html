One of the basement windows is wide open. It's small but you think you might be able to wriggle through. You stick your head inside and look around. You can't see much except cobwebs and dust. As you start working your arm through the window a loud creak alerts you to the presence of something nearby. You panic and start to pull back causing you to get jammed in the window frame. Looking up you see a lumpy shape easing toward you. Screaming you pull back harder, but it's no use, you're trapped. The lumpy shape grabs hold of you, the pain in your neck intensifies until your head is torn from your body.
